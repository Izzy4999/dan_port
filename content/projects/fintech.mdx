## Fintech App

Welcome to our simple fintech app built with React Native. Manage your finances and stay updated on cryptocurrency market trends.

### Features

- **Customizable Card Layout:** Arrange cards according to your preferences for easy access to information.
- **Real-Time Crypto Market Graphs:** Stay informed with current market graphs for Bitcoin, Ethereum, BNB, and more.
- **Secure Authentication:** Login securely to access your financial data.
- **Personalized Alerts:** Set up alerts for price changes and important market events.
- **User-Friendly Interface:** Intuitive design for seamless navigation and user experience.

### How It Works

1. **Login or Sign Up:** Start by creating an account or logging in to your existing one.
2. **Arrange Cards:** Customize your dashboard by arranging cards to prioritize the information you need.
3. **View Crypto Market Graphs:** Check the latest market trends for cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, BNB, and more.
4. \*\*Unlock with faceid, code and fingerprint.
